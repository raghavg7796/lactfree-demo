Privacy, plainly.

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are. LactFree is a lactose-free dairy brand preparing to launch in Bangalore, India. This site exists to introduce our first products and let you join our launch list. Contact: hello@lactfree.in

What we collect

  • Nothing you type on this site. There are no forms or accounts here, and we take no payment on this site.
  • Basic analytics. We use the Meta Pixel to understand how visitors use these pages (pages viewed, buttons tapped). This may use cookies and is subject to Meta's privacy policy.
  • What you choose to share. If you tap "Join on WhatsApp," you open a chat with us on WhatsApp and share the number and name you use there. If you type your pincode in the optional box, the full pincode is included in that WhatsApp message so we can plan delivery areas; our analytics only ever receives the first three digits, never the full pincode. If you use our form instead, we receive what you enter (handled by Tally).

How we use it

Only to tell you when LactFree launches in your area, honour launch-list offers (like your free first litre), and ask the occasional one-line question about what you'd like us to make. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Launch offer terms

"First litre free" / "first bottle free": one free unit per person, for people who join the launch list before delivery opens, redeemable with your first order once LactFree delivers in your area of Bangalore. No cash value, not transferable. If we can't serve your area at launch, the offer holds until we can, or you can simply leave the list.

Opting out

Message "STOP" on WhatsApp any time and we'll remove you from the list. For anything else, write to hello@lactfree.in and we'll sort it out.
